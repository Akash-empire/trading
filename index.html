Things We Trade

Crypto Trade

Crypto Trade is the act of buying and selling digital currencies like Bitcoin to profit from price changes.

Shares Trade

Share market Trade is the buying and selling of publicly traded company shares on a stock exchange to make a profit. This is done by investors who analyze market trends, company performance, and economic factors.

Gold Trade

There are three main ways to trade metals: on the spot market, via futures and options, or as a forex pair. When commodity consumers want to purchase goods for immediate delivery, they'll take to the spot market.

Currency Trade

Currency Trade, also known as forex or foreign exchange Trade, is the process of buying one currency and simultaneously selling another with the goal of profiting from fluctuations in their exchange rates.

Silver Trade

Silver Trade involves speculating on the price of silver through various instruments like futures, spot prices, ETFs, and CFDs, rather than buying physical silver.

How Trade Works

Understanding the Market

Trade begins with accessing a financial market—such as stocks, forex, commodities, or cryptocurrencies—where buyers and sellers exchange assets.

Placing Trades and Managing Positions

Once a trader identifies an opportunity, they place an order through a Trade platform. This can be a buy order (expecting the price to rise) or a sell order (expecting it to fall).

Profiting Through Market Movement

Profit in Trade is earned when the market moves in the trader’s favor. For example, buying low and selling high generates profit in an uptrend, while selling high and buying back lower works in a downtrend.
